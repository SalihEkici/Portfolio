Internship

Title: SKILLSEEKER

From February 24 2024 to May 29 2024, I interned at Mediaan Conclusian a consultancy company in Hasselt Corda Campus.

Mediaan Conclusion, an IT consultancy company with offices in Belgium, the Netherlands, and Germany, recently joined the Conclusion group and rebranded as Mediaan Conclusion. They were facing a significant challenge in efficiently matching employees to projects. The traditional methods of assigning employees to projects were time-consuming and often ineffective, resulting in suboptimal project outcomes and employee dissatisfaction. Recognizing the urgency of this issue, Mediaan Conclusion sought a solution that could streamline this process.

They requested a sophisticated application designed to address their needs. This system was envisioned to not only find the best employees for a given project based on their skills but also to suggest employees who could benefit from learning new skills relevant to the project. The goal was to enhance resource allocation and promote continuous skill development within the organization.

The product developed for Mediaan Conclusion simplifies employee management for the sales team. When a new project is offered, it can be entered into the application, and suitable employees are matched to it. A key feature of the application is its ability to recommend employees with related skills. For instance, if a project requires Java experience, the system can also suggest C# developers due to the similarity between the two languages. This is achieved through a graph database which allows us to generate ratings for every employee based on the selected skill and the similarities to that skill.

The application allows employees to self-assess their skills on a scale from 1 to 10. These ratings help the system create a detailed skill profile for each employee, enabling precise matching of skills to project requirements. This approach ensures that employees are not only placed in projects where they can excel but also in roles where they have opportunities for growth and learning.

Contributions

  • Backend Development: Worked on developing the backend APIs that power the application.
  • Frontend Development: Contributed to the design and development of the user-friendly frontend interface.
  • Rating System: Contirbuted to the implementation of the rating system using the Neo4j graph database to establish relationships between skills and employees based on their self-assessed scores.
